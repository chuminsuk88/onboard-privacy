Privacy Policy

OnBoard by Blue Boppy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use OnBoard, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your email address and name from Google sign-in
  • Calendar Data: Events from your Google Calendar and Apple Calendar
  • Email Data: Event information extracted from your Gmail inbox (with your permission)
  • Location Information: Your home address and event locations for travel time calculations
  • Family Information: Names of family members you add
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the OnBoard service
  • Generate your daily schedule digest
  • Calculate travel times and send notifications
  • Sync events across your calendars
  • Provide weather forecasts for events
  • Enable family schedule sharing
  • Improve the app

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only when:

  • With Family Members: Events you choose to share with family members in the app
  • Service Providers: Google (authentication, calendar, email), Apple (calendar), weather services, mapping services, and cloud hosting
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

4. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Secure Google OAuth 2.0 authentication

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app settings
  • Update your information in the app
  • Delete your account by contacting us
  • Revoke calendar/email access through your Google or Apple account
  • Disable location or notifications in your device settings
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Data Retention

We keep your data while you use OnBoard. When you delete your account, we delete your data within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

OnBoard uses:

  • Google: Authentication, Gmail, Google Calendar - Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Apple Calendar - Apple Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

OnBoard is for adults managing family schedules. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use means you accept the changes.
